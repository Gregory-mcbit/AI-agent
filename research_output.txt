Main Problems with Land Rover Discovery 4:
1. Timing Chain Issues - Common in 2010-2016 models leading to starting and stalling problems.
2. Oil Leaks - Often related to timing chain failures, resulting in oil contamination or low oil pressure.
3. Transmission Problems - Frequently reported, particularly in older models.
4. Engine Overheating - Diesel engines like TDV6 and SDV6 may run hot, indicating potential issues.
5. Electrical System Issues - Reports of electrical failures in later models (2017-2020).
6. Air Suspension Problems - Known for causing discomfort and handling issues.

These problems can often lead to reduced reliability and higher maintenance costs.
